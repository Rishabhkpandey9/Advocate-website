About Advocate Pandey

Advocate Sachindra Kumar Pandey is a distinguished legal professional with over 40 years of experience in Criminal, Civil, and Family Law. Practicing in Daltonganj Palamau Jharkhand and neighboring districts, he is renowned for his deep knowledge, courtroom expertise, and ethical approach to justice.

As a Senior Advocate, he has handled a wide range of high-profile cases with unmatched dedication and precision. His clients trust him for honest advice, sharp legal strategies, and a calm, result-oriented presence in the courtroom.

He is a member of the Jharkhand Bar Association and continues to guide young lawyers while actively participating in legal awareness initiatives.
